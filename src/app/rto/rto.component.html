Sounpak® HVAC Silencers


McGill AirFlow offers a complete line of Sounpak silencers for noise control HVAC applications. Sounpak silencers have been designed, tested, and manufactured to ensure the highest performance and most efficient operation at an economical price.

McGill AirFlow has conducted acoustical testing programs on all Sounpak silencers. As a result of these tests, (run in accordance with ASTM standard E477), reliable and accurate performance data is available. This data allows the performance of Sounpak silencers to be easily and accurately predicted, and makes it possible to select the proper model and size to meet specified noise reduction requirements.

The performance ratings are available in easy-to-use tabular form by contacting McGill AirFlow.

Sounpak® HVAC Silencer Construction


Specialized construction features are available for all McGill AirFlow rectangular and round silencers.

Material

Galvanized, paintable galvanized, stainless steel Types 304 and 316, and aluminum


Material Thickness

10- to 22-gauge outer shells
18- to 24-gauge perforated inner baffles


Connections

Flanges, transitions, slip-fit, raw, applied connectors


Acoustical-grade Fill Barriers

Mylar®, Tedlar®, polyethylene, and fiberglass cloth


Acoustical-fill

Standard acoustical-grade insulation, high-temperature acoustical-grade insulation

Circular (round) silencers are available in standard inner diameters of 12 to 60 inches. The length varies as a condition of pressure and insertion loss requirements. Applications include low- to high-velocity systems. Passive silencers can be ordered with a vapor barrier between the fill and the baffle wall. Circular silencers are available in galvanized steel, paintable steel, 304 and 316 stainless steels, and aluminum.
