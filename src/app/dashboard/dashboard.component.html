Complete Turnkey Service for Your Air Pollution Control Needs

We can start by using our mobile testing equipment to analyze your emissions and size a full-scale system for your application. Then, depending upon your emission control needs, we can supply a system that combines any or all of the following:

  • Electrostatic precipitators, fabric filters, and ceramic filters for particulate removal
  • Regenerative thermal oxidizers for control of volatile organic compounds
  • DeNOx SCR reactors and ceramic filters for removal of NOx
  • Reagent storage, reagent preparation, reagent transport, spray-dry scrubbers, and reaction chambers for acid gas control
  • Auxiliary equipment like evaporative coolers, ductwork, control systems, and stacks for complete system operation

We can handle construction of your system, supplying all of the necessary equipment. Finally, once installation has been completed, we will work with you to make sure your process meets emissions standards now, and in the future.
