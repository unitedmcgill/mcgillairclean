Catalytic Filter System


McGill AirClean has been manufacturing fabric filter (baghouse) systems since 1991. Recently, we have modified our fabric filter system design to include the use of high temperature (750oF) ceramic catalytic filters. The catalytic filters remove both particulate and NOx (with ammonia injection), and provide a secondary reaction site for acid gas reduction (SOx, HCl, HF, and B2O3). We currently have 3 systems operating, and 3 under construction. Please feel free to reach out under the “Contact Us” section for more information.
